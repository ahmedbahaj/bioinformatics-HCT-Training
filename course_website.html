About this course

What we'll explore together over two weekends.

This short course is your first real step into bioinformatics — the computational study of biological data. Over the next two weekends we'll cover what biological sequences are, how they're stored, and how to analyze them using tools that clinicians, researchers, and engineers use every day.

No prior programming or biology background is assumed. We'll use Google Colab for all hands-on work, so you only need a laptop and a Google account.
